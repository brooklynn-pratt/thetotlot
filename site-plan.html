Overview

Purpose

My idea for the website would be a place to help small childcare businesses. This website would basically be a big folder that makes it super simple to figure out certain kids’ allergies and preferences to foods and other things. Teachers would be able to add different categories to their class, one for different food allergies, one for medications, and another for alternative activities or meals for kids that may not get to participate

Audience

Teachers and parents who have kids in daycares

Content

Home page

a grid of all the teachers at whatever daycare or school is using this site, all the pictures show up with names underneath, and when you hover your mouse over a teacher it will have a hover effect and you will be able to click on the picture.

Images for the Home page

Miss John's Class

All kids names and ages and pictures are organized with the teachers name at the top and in alphabetical order so that each kid is displayed in a clear and organized way so it is easy to navigate. When you hover your mouse over a childs picture it will have a glow behind it, and you are able to click on it and pull up that Childs notes and health information.
